HOW IT WORKS
Four steps from raw data to confident bet — every race day, automatically.
1
Data Ingested
EveryRace sectional CSV is downloaded automatically at 7am, 10am, 12pm and 2pm. Scratchings are cross-referenced against the TAB API in real time.
2
Horses Scored
Each runner gets a score out of 248 combining speed percentiles, form metrics, track conditions, and connections data — weighted by your custom sliders.
3
Odds Priced
Scores feed into a bookmaker-calibrated pricing matrix to generate fair market odds for every runner in the race field.
4
Value Found
Our price is compared against live TAB SP. When the market significantly overestimates a Rank 1 horse, that's your bet — flagged and ready to act on.
